What it is

Built for the moment when everything hurts.

When a migraine hits, the last thing you want is a complicated interface. MigrainSpace puts a quiet, dark, immersive environment one tap away on Apple Vision Pro — with optional ambient sound and breathing guidance to help you through it.

It also tracks your sessions so you can spot patterns over time and share a clean summary with your doctor. No setup, no accounts, no friction.

What you get
Dark Space
A fully immersive, low-light environment that replaces your surroundings. Gentle enough for light-sensitive moments.
Ambient Sound
Soft, looping audio designed to reduce sensory overload — rain, low drones, or silence. You choose.
Breathing Guidance
Optional slow breathing prompts to activate the parasympathetic response and reduce pain intensity.
Session Tracking
Every session is logged — duration, time of day, and intensity. No input required during an episode.
Pattern Recognition
See your migraine history over weeks and months. Spot triggers, frequencies, and durations at a glance.
Doctor Summary
Export a clean, readable summary of your session history to share at your next appointment.
