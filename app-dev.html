App Development

For 13 years at Apple, any app I built on my own time would have become Apple's IP, so I didn't build apps. I thought about building apps and accumulated a lot of ideas with nowhere to put them. Getting laid off in early 2026 allowed me to finally work on this stuff. It's been really fun to learn again.

FieldSense was the first thing I worked on when I had the time. I'd had the idea for a while. I'm learning coding to build it and honestly the learning part has been the most fun.

visionOS

MigrainSpace

MigrainSpace is for Apple Vision Pro. Migraines make screens painful and thinking hard, so it's designed for exactly that moment. One tap puts you in a quiet, dark space with ambient sound and optional breathing guidance. It also tracks your sessions so you can spot patterns and share a summary with your doctor.
