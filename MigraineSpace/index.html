FAQ

Is my data private?

Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device and is never shared with third parties.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete all app data by uninstalling MigraineSpace from your device, or by using the reset option in Settings within the app.

I found a bug. What should I do?

Please email beng@me.com with a description of the issue along with your device model and visionOS version.
